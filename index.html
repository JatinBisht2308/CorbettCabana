Welcome to Corbett Cabana Farm

Cozy haven nestled in the heart of Corbett

Discover Corbett Cabana Farm, your tranquil oasis nestled in 7 km dense forest area near Nainital. Our boutique resort offers an exclusive blend of luxury and nature, ensuring every guest enjoys privacy, relaxation, and authentic hospitality. Wake up to stunning views of lush greenery, the sound of birdsong, and fresh mountain air. Each elegantly designed room is equipped for comfort and serenity, perfect for couples, families, or solo travelers seeking a peaceful getaway.

Adventure & Trekking

Discover the Trails Beyond Our Farm

From Corbett Cabana Farm, embark on a unique trekking route through dense forest: first to the ancient Hathikhal Shiva Temple nestled in rocky caves (5 km), then on to the picturesque Hathikhal Waterfall (9 km), and all the way to Nainital along the winding river. Whether spiritual serenity or wild adventure is your goal, this journey offers unforgettable experiences amidst pristine nature.
