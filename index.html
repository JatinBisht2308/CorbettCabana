Welcome to Corbett Cabana

Cozy haven nestled in the heart of Corbett

Discover Corbett Cabana, your tranquil oasis nestled in the heart of Jim Corbett National Park. Our boutique resort offers an exclusive blend of luxury and nature, ensuring every guest enjoys privacy, relaxation, and authentic hospitality. Wake up to stunning views of lush greenery, the sound of birdsong, and fresh mountain air. Each elegantly designed room is equipped for comfort and serenity, perfect for couples, families, or solo travelers seeking a peaceful getaway.
