About Corbett Cabana Farm

The Jungle Heaven in the Heart of Corbett

Corbett Cabana Farm is your private escape amid the wild charm of Jim Corbett National Park. Our boutique property blends eco-luxury and rustic character, offering an immersive nature experience with all modern indulgence. Relax in beautifully appointed rooms, rise to the melody of birds, and breathe the pure mountain air—every moment is crafted for tranquility, adventure, and genuine warmth.

  • Spacious, thoughtfully designed cabanas with jungle/garden views
  • Located inside a 7 km stretch of dense forest near Nainital for a true wilderness experience
  • Direct access to scenic trekking routes—including the ancient Hathikhal Shiva Temple, Hathikhal Waterfall, and onward to Nainital
  • Organic dining celebrating local flavors
  • Refresh at the pool, rejuvenate with Jacuzzi, or cozy up by the bonfire
  • Guided safaris, nature walks, and village experiences arranged on request
  • Eco-friendly practices and personalized hospitality
  • Adventure activities for all ages: birdwatching, river walks, campfires, storytelling evenings
